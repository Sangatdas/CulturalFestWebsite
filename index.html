About Us.

Manzar is the annual cultural festival of the Institute of Chemical technology. It has completed 10 years since it first began in 2007, and 2016 saw the 10th edition of this festival. Every year, Manzar proudly hosts a variety of events in music, dance, literary arts and fine arts.

The Dance and Fashion Show events are the most popular, with active participation of students from all over the city.

AWAAZ

-The Social Initiative



AWAAZ Is the flagship social initiative associated with Manzar which has achieved great success. We have been successful in showing that at Manzar, care and teach our students the importance of community service. Manzar has also had the privilege of being a part of the renowned comedy club AIB on its show '' On Air with AIB'' in 2015.
